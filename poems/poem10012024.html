A Love Poem for Christina

On this Tuesday's gentle morn,
My heart to you is sworn.
In every whisper of the breeze,
I find your love, my sweetest ease.

Your smile, a beacon, bright and true,
Guides me in all I do.
With each day, my love does grow,
In you, my dearest, I find my glow.

So on this Tuesday, I declare,
My love for you beyond compare.
Stephanie, my heart's delight,
You make my world forever bright.
