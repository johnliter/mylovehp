A Love Poem for Christina

On this Tuesday's gentle morn, My heart to you is sworn. In every whisper of the breeze, I find your love, my sweetest ease.
Your smile, a beacon, bright and true, Guides me in all I do. With each day, my love does grow, In you, my dearest, I find my glow.
So on this Tuesday, I declare, My love for you beyond compare. Christina, my heart's delight, You make my world forever bright.

"I love you more each and every day, Christina!"

Created on October 01, 2024, for my one true love, Christina.

"Every beat of my heart belongs to you."
